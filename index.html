Privacy Policy

Sugar Pulse LLC

This Privacy Policy describes how we, Sugar Pulse LLC, collect, use, and protect your personal information when using our site and services, including registration. We value your privacy and guarantee that your data is protected in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

1. Collection and Use of Information

We collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide our services:

  • Phone Number: When registering, we only use your phone number to identify you as a user, send you a one-time password (OTP) to confirm your identity, and grant you access to the site’s functionality.

2. We Do Not Store Your Phone Number

We do not store users’ phone numbers in our database. We use the number exclusively for:

  • User identification.
  • Sending a one-time password (OTP) for login verification.

After successful authorization, the phone number is not saved in our system.

3. Use of Information

The collected information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • User identification during registration and login.
  • Sending a one-time password (OTP) to verify identity.
  • Ensuring the correct operation of the site and providing necessary services.

4. Data Security

We take all possible measures to protect users’ data, including:

  • Encryption of transmitted information.
  • Compliance with security standards and best practices.

5. Data Transfer to Third Parties

We do not share your phone number or other personal data with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • When required by law.
  • When necessary for the provision of services, such as interacting with Telegram services to send OTPs.

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about the data we collect and process about you.
  • Request deletion or correction of your data.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be published on this page. Please check it periodically to stay updated on the latest terms and conditions.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions or comments regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us via Telegram technical support.
